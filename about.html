Meet the creators

A Message From Us

Hello from the owners of the Winter Stop N Shop. Our goal is to share our personal collection of clothing to the world and give people a chance to make a purchase. Everything found on the website comes straight from our closets. We change the items sold every season and this season is Winter Season. We have been into clothing every since we were growing up. Our goal is not to sell the most expensive or highest quality item to you. It is to sell you something that can not be found anywhere else on the market for cheaper! If you see something cheaper, show us and we will beat it! That's the kind of business we run.



Because of limited quantity, only 1 purchase per customer per season!!

Our Mission

We donate 50% of our profit to Second Harvest Food Bank of Mahoning Valley in Youngstown, Ohio. We are locals to Ohio and love to support our communities. The Second Harvest Food Bank gives food to people all around Mahoning Valley. This was close to the home of one of the owners. He always brought food their as a kid and helped distribute the food. You can also click here to visit their site and donate directly. Send the owner proof of donations and see what comes in the mail!
